# Smart Calculator

A versatile and feature-rich calculator application built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This calculator goes beyond basic arithmetic, offering a wide range of functionalities to cater to various needs, from students to professionals.

## ‚ú® Features

The Smart Calculator boasts a modular design, with different modes accessible through a user-friendly sidebar navigation:

*   **Standard Calculator:** For your everyday arithmetic needs.
*   **Scientific Calculator:** Packed with functions like sine, cosine, tangent, logarithm, square root, and more. It also includes constants like œÄ (pi) and e.
*   **Graphing Calculator:** Visualize mathematical equations. Simply enter an equation (e.g., `y = x^2`) and see it plotted on a graph.
*   **Converter:** A handy tool for various unit conversions.
*   **Financial Calculator:** A suite of tools for financial calculations:
    *   **EMI Calculator:** Calculate Equated Monthly Installments for loans.
    *   **GST Calculator:** Easily add or subtract Goods and Services Tax.
    *   **Income Tax Calculator:** Get a quick estimate of your income tax.
    *   **SIP Calculator:** Plan your Systematic Investment Plan investments.
    *   **Currency Converter:** Convert between different currencies.
*   **Programming Calculator:** A tool for programmers with features like:
    *   **Base Converter:** Convert numbers between decimal, hexadecimal, octal, and binary.
    *   **Bitwise Operations:** Perform bitwise AND, OR, XOR, NOT, and shift operations.
*   **AI Solver:** Ask any math question or general query and get an instant answer. It also supports voice input in multiple languages.
*   **History:** Keep track of your previous calculations. You can search your history and even export it as a CSV or PDF file.
